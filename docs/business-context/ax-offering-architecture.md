# **3\. The Capability Curve**

The Capability Curve is AX’s core methodology. It describes the journey from initial AI adoption through to organisation-wide transformation. It is both a diagnostic tool (to assess where a client currently sits) and a development framework (to define what they need next).

Where any given client starts on the curve depends on four inputs: business context, people skills, problems to solve and their business drivers, and current maturity level.

| Level | Name | What This Means |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **5** | **Scale** | Organisation-wide. AI is embedded in how work gets done. Self-sustaining without external support. |
| **4** | **Automate & Combine** | Connecting systems and stringing workflows together. Multiple tools and processes working in concert. |
| **3** | **Codify** | Building reusable assets: skills, custom GPTs, project templates, prompt libraries. Capturing knowledge so it scales beyond the individual. |
| **2** | **System & Plan** *(The Centrepiece)* | Using AI as a genuine thinking partner. Planning before interacting. This is where the real value begins. Most competitors stop before reaching this level. This is what separates AX’s approach from tool-adoption training. |
| **1** | **Context & Communicate** | Set-up, personalisation, teaching the tools about you and your work. Prompting frameworks (CRISP). Learning how to communicate effectively with AI. |

## **Frameworks Within the Curve**

* **CRISP** sits within Context & Communicate as the prompting methodology.

* **DOTS** (Dream Outcomes, Obstacles, Triage, Sequencing) is the use case discovery and prioritisation tool, used primarily in workshops and the System & Plan level.

* **TSS** (Tools x Skills x Systems) is the underlying logic of the entire curve. Not client-facing, but it explains why the curve works: at each level, all three dimensions advance together.

# **5\. Offering Architecture**

Three tiers of offering, each serving a different moment in the client journey. All offerings are contextualised to the client’s business, not generic.

**Team Bootcamp**

| Format | 6–10 weeks. Single company. |
| :---- | :---- |

## **Tier 3: Organisational Enablement**

The big-ticket, most differentiated offering. A full transformation partnership delivered on a monthly retainer, typically over 6–18 months. This is the Altima model made repeatable.

**How It Works**

**Starts with leadership alignment** to establish shared mental models, terminology, and a clear strategic direction. Then builds capability across the whole organisation, mapped to specific teams, use cases, and maturity levels. The delivery cadence and medium are defined with the client.

**Delivery Mechanisms (selected with the client, not all at once)**

* In-person half-day immersives

* Virtual workshops

* Virtual webinars

* On-demand learning via Thrive platform

* 1:1 coaching for leaders

* Fractional Chief AI Officer (CAIO) – embedded

* Programme management and reporting (KPIs and ROI)

* Train the trainer / AI champions development

* Done-for-you and done-with-you development cycles (workflow and tool builds)

* Online access and community

**Key Principles**

* Problem first, not tools.

* Cultural adoption, not just skills.

* We get inside the DNA of the organisation.

* We define the cadence and medium of rollout specific to you.

* Raise the baseline, then accelerate aligned to vision and strategy.

* Enablement programme is bespoke: by role, by goal, by team, by use case.

* We train AI champions who sustain the transformation internally.

* We are partners until the job is done. Then the capability belongs to you.

**Organisational Enablement Journey**

| \# | Stage | What Happens |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| 1 | **Leadership Alignment** | DOTS framework with senior leadership. Shared vision, strategic priorities, terminology, and accountability established. |
| 2 | **Capability Dev & Culture Adoption** | Build capability across the organisation mapped to teams, roles, use cases, and maturity levels. Embed AI into the culture and ways of working. |
| 3 | **Focused Cycles** | Sprint-based delivery against highest-priority use cases. Accelerate progress with hands-on support for workflow builds, automation, and tool integration. |
| 4 | **Partner / Coach / Support** | Ongoing partnership, training of internal champions, and transition to full internal ownership. We step back when the job is done. |
