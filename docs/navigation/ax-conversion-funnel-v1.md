# AX Conversion Funnel v1 (Urgent Launch)

*Version 1.0 — March 2026*  
*Status: Step-1 launch plan (clarity + signup + notifications)*

---

## 1) Purpose

Define the minimum complete funnel needed now to:

1. sell what AX does and why it matters
2. enable qualified users to sign up/apply
3. notify AX team immediately after submission

This is the launch architecture for urgent demand capture (workshops + next cohort).

---

## 2) Funnel objective and constraints

### Primary objective

Convert current demand into structured inbound via clear offer pages and reliable forms.

### Time constraint

Next paid cohort launch window: 2-3 weeks.

### Scope boundary (Step 1)

In scope now:

- information architecture and page-level conversion flow
- high-quality landing page content structure
- form capture and internal notifications

Out of scope for Step 1:

- payment processing integration (e.g., GoHighLevel)
- advanced CRM orchestration and lifecycle automation
- complex account/portal functionality

---

## 3) Funnel map (v1)

## Entry points

- Homepage primary pathways
- Promo banner (global, reusable)
- Relevant insights/article CTAs
- Direct social/email links
- Returning users via direct URL

## Priority destinations

1. Workshop page (Leadership Activation)
2. Cohort page (next intake)
3. Quiz destination (`https://quiz.accelerator-x.ai`)

## Conversion actions

- Workshop CTA: apply/book activation
- Cohort CTA: join waitlist/apply for next run
- Quiz CTA: take diagnostic and continue journey

## Post-submit states

- On-page success confirmation
- clear expectation for response time
- internal notification to AX team

---

## 4) Required pages and page roles

### A) What We Do page (clarity engine)

Role:

- explain AX model, differentiation, and who it is for

Must include:

- clear positioning and audience fit
- how to work with AX (3 pathways)
- explicit links to workshop, cohort, quiz

Primary CTA:

- "Find your best starting point"

### B) Workshop page (Leadership Activation)

Role:

- convert workshop-intent traffic and qualify serious buyers

Must include:

- who this is for/not for
- outcomes and process
- website pricing line: **From £2,000 + VAT**
- what happens after inquiry

Primary CTA:

- "Apply for Leadership Activation"

### C) Cohort page (next intake)

Role:

- convert high-intent cohort demand quickly

Must include:

- cohort promise and outcomes
- fit criteria and commitment expectations
- intake timing and cohort status
- VSL section
- incentive logic (e.g., included course access)

Primary CTA:

- "Apply for next cohort"

### D) Quiz routing treatment

Role:

- discoverability and intent segmentation

Must include (across site):

- global banner entry
- contextual links from relevant pages
- clear handoff from quiz outcomes to workshop/cohort pages

---

## 5) Navigation and discoverability requirements

- All three priority destinations reachable in 1 click from primary navigation logic.
- Maximum 2 clicks to reach workshop or cohort from homepage and insights pages.
- Global reusable promo banner component enabled, with campaign-level controls.

Initial banner campaign:

- "Take the AI readiness quiz" -> `https://quiz.accelerator-x.ai`

Future campaign slots:

- next cohort intake
- event launch
- strategic seasonal campaigns

---

## 6) Form and notification architecture (Step 1)

### Form data (minimum viable)

Required fields:

- full name
- work email
- role/title
- company
- primary interest (workshop/cohort/other)
- brief context/challenge

Optional fields:

- phone
- preferred timing

### Routing rules

- Workshop form -> workshop pipeline tag/label
- Cohort form -> cohort pipeline tag/label
- Quiz referrals -> source attribution where possible

### Notifications

On submit:

- internal Slack notification (lead summary + source)
- email confirmation/log for internal follow-up

User-facing confirmation:

- success state with expected follow-up timeline

---

## 7) Messaging and CTA standards

- One dominant CTA per page
- No mixed high-friction and low-friction primary asks on same viewport
- Value and fit context before form ask
- CTA labels must be explicit: apply/join/book (avoid vague "learn more" as primary)

---

## 8) Instrumentation (v1)

Track at minimum:

- traffic source -> destination page
- CTA click-through by page
- form starts and completions
- submission success/failure events
- quiz click-through and return paths

Dashboard questions to answer weekly:

1. Which entry points create qualified submissions?
2. Where do users drop before submission?
3. Is cohort page converting urgent demand effectively?
4. Is quiz discoverability improving?

---

## 9) Launch checklist (Step 1)

Before launch:

- workshop page complete and quality-reviewed
- cohort page complete and quality-reviewed
- form submission and notifications tested end-to-end
- banner active and linked correctly
- at least one insights-to-offer contextual CTA live

After launch (first 7 days):

- daily check submission pipeline and notification reliability
- monitor CTA performance and section-level drop-off
- tune copy and CTA placement based on behavior

---

## 10) Step-2 handoff (future)

Candidate upgrades after Step 1 is stable:

- GoHighLevel/payment integration
- enhanced CRM automations and scoring
- richer segmentation and nurture logic
- deeper Slack workflows and internal triage automation

Step 2 should only start once Step 1 pages are live and reliably converting.
